Meet the Team

We are a passionate group of developers, designers, and builders behind the Moffat Bay Lodge web application. Our diverse backgrounds and collaborative spirit allowed us to create a seamless digital experience for guests, staff, and administrators alike.

Steve Stylin
Database Administrator & Backend Developer
Steve was born in Haiti and holds a degree in Computer Science. She began her career as an Analyst Programmer for four years before being promoted to Junior Database Administrator, and three years later advancing to Senior DBA in Haiti. Currently, Steve is expanding her expertise at Bellevue University, pursuing studies in Software Development to gain a broader perspective in the field.

Key Contributions:
  • Designed the Test-Driven Development (TDD) approach for the project
  • Created the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) for the application database
  • Developed the backend of the landing page
  • Leading the development of the About Us page (frontend and backend)
Fun Fact: Steve loves hiking and camping. Her favorite authors include Eckhart Tolle, Stephen King, Edgar Allan Poe, and Nicholas Sparks.
